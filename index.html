About

The TRIVIALIZER is an application designed, produced, and deployed by Evan Boileau, Rekha Leelara, Vijay Rao, Oleg Dobrovolskyi, and Charles Johnston as the first group project assigned for the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, done through the University of Toronto’s School of Continuing Studies program.

This timed trivia quiz was conceived and created between the dates of Jan 27th and Feb 13th, 2022 - a game designed to give the user control over the tailor-made design of a trivia quiz by picking a genre (between movies, science, history, sports, music, television, books and geography) and a difficulty setting (between easy, medium, and hard).

The motivation behind the game was to give users something with substance to do during their idle time between tasks or errands, a work-out for the brain, a ‘tech happy hour’, something to reduce stress, or just something to use to have a bit of fun.

The zeitgeist of the project is the current COVID crisis and looking for ways to delight people while also giving them a sense of control.
